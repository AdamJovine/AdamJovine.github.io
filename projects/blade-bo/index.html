BLADE‑BO

Quality diversity optimization for exam scheduling.

Visit Project Site

BLADE‑BO introduces quality diversity principles to Bayesian Optimization for improved exam scheduling.This approach generates diverse, high‑quality solutions that balance multiple competing objectives in university scheduling scenarios.We developed a new method that actively balances time spent on each stage of the algorithm, avoiding hyperparameter sensitivity common in cost‑aware sequential‑BO techniques like LAMBO and CarBO.
